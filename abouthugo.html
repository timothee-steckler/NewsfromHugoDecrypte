Who is Hugo Decrypte?

Hugo Decrypte

Hugo Decrypte is a French Youtuber and journalist. He started a media held by young people in 2015 called Radio Londres along with diverse Youtube channels affiliated to Radio Londres.
He really got famous during the 2017 presidential election as he interviewed different candidates. These got very famous and were used for the public to understand the different points of views and promises.
4 years later, his company employs 13 people And his YouTube channels has now about 3 millions followers.
